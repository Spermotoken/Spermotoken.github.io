When a token is "in circulation," it means that all of the tokens that have been created or minted are available for buying, selling, and trading on the market. In contrast, if some of the tokens are held by the project team or other parties and are not available for trading, then those tokens are considered to be "out of circulation."

In this context, the statement "we aim to make the total supply of the token 100% in circulation" means that the creators of the token want all of the tokens that they create to be immediately available for buying, selling, and trading on the market. This would mean that there are no tokens that are held back, reserved, or locked up by the team or any other parties.

Additionally, the statement mentions that the distribution of the tokens will be "fair." This implies that the distribution of the tokens will not be biased towards any particular group or individual. Instead, the tokens will be distributed in a way that is equitable and accessible to all interested parties.

Overall, the goal of making the total supply of the token 100% in circulation and fairly distributed is to create a more transparent and equitable token economy, where everyone has a fair opportunity to participate and benefit from the token.
